# chzh

Data produced by Canton of Zürich, Amt für Raumentwicklung, Abteilung Geoinformation.


## License

The [dataset description](https://geo.zh.ch/data/datasets/9dbb9f87-dd0a-4685-8a86-9d2073643766)
states “OGD — freie Nutzung und Weitergabe” (Open Government Data – free use and redistribution)
without indicating a specific license. On April 29, 2026, we asked the cantonal GIS department
for clarification. In their reply, they pointed us to the City of Zürich, whose Open Data Portal
[re-distributes](https://data.stadt-zuerich.ch/dataset/ktzh_digitales_terrainmodell__dtm__2021_bis_2022__ogd_)
the full cantonal dataset under Creative Common Zero (CC0). We still download the DTM data files
from the cantonal website because it is technically much easier (the city portal has an ordering
process to generate temporary download links, sent over email, whereas the canton has a simple
webserver). The spatial coverage is the same, no matter whether we take the data from the city
or the canton.
